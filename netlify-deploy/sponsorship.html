Heritage H2GP Seeks Strategic Partners for Hydrogen Racing Program

By Likith Tatini September 13, 2025
Heritage High School's acclaimed H2GP (Hydrogen Grand Prix) racing team is revolutionizing sustainable transportation education through cutting-edge hydrogen-powered RC car competition. With over 50 active members and 15+ competition victories, our STEAM club represents the future of clean energy innovation.

Led by faculty advisor Mr. Pennington, the Heritage H2GP team has established itself as a powerhouse in the H2GP competition, consistently achieving top rankings at regional, state, and international levels. Our comprehensive STEAM education program integrates Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics through practical application of sustainable energy systems.

"We're not just racing cars ‚Äì we're engineering the future of sustainable transportation," says Mr. Pennington. "Our students gain hands-on experience with hydrogen fuel cell technology while developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork skills essential for 21st-century careers."
