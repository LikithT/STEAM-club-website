Beyond Racing

Within Innovation

Heritage H2GP transforms hydrogen-powered RC car racing into comprehensive STEAM education, inspiring the next generation of sustainable energy innovators.

Heritage H2GP is a distinguished STEAM education club that transforms hydrogen-powered RC car racing into a comprehensive learning experience. We inspire students to apply scientific and engineering principles through hands-on experimentation, competing at regional, state, and international levels.

Our mission is to cultivate innovation and foster a passion for sustainable energy solutions among our peers, preparing the next generation of leaders for successful careers in STEM fields.

Hydrogen-Powered RC Cars
